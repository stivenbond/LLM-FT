Burn the ships The man is said to have bravery and power. The stories write of heros on stones, —Stones that are carved over and over only for heros and gods. But humans are not always what stories say. They have no super strength to move mountains, and there is no such sane man on earth, who'd walk all certain in an unknown island. It is difficult to read between words, when the stories all blur one another. So don't be saying to me they were sure! They weren't destined to have such power! I am not sure of what it will bring... for paths all are rough and towered, but still I'll make the ships burn, so I’ll have to look forward.
